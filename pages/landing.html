About us

We are a group of seven students that came together to create a web application with our love for music. The Music Match web application allows users to visually see their music preferences along with those of other users, by letting them search for Artists, Albums, or Songs and see recommended results. With our extensive catalogue of music, we allow enjoyers of music to extend their own catalogue by finding music they may have never found if not for our website. Our hope with this app is to allow fans of music to find a new listen or for artist to find new inspiration. MusicMatch is the place you come to match the music you enjoy.
